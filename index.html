    Food for a family


    About 26,000 packets of dry and other food items have been planned for the victims floods. Each packet of food containing rice-pulses-oil-salt sugar is expected to last a week for a family of five. At the same time cash and rice have also been planned.

    Shelter for a family


    We are planned to facilities include a community room for those without shelter, tube-wells, latrines, fishponds, and areas for vegetable cultivation and trees plantations. Also provide boats as the risk of flood increases the rescue boats are located around the areas most at risk, and/or at flood shelters.

    Providing Medical Relief


    The impact of the medical camp was phenomenal. As we set out on this journey, the task this big is not possible for us alone, without your combined help. Only some contribute can help us ease their struggles with not only food reliefs but also medical as well as long term sustainable assistance with farming.
